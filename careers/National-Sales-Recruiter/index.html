    • National Sales Recruiter (Contract)

    We are looking for a talented and experienced national sales recruiter to join our team and help us sale SquareResults (RADAR) Recruiting Services. You will be responsible for sourcing, screening, and sharing with candidates for various recruiting products across the country. You will also work closely with Chief People Officer to understand the needs and expectations, and to ensure a smooth and efficient sales process.


    About the role

    • As a national sales recruiter, you will be part of a dynamic and successful team that sells SquareResults (RADAR) Recruiting Services to candidates across the country.
    • You will use your sales skills and recruitment knowledge to identify, attract, and engage with qualified candidates for various recruiting products.
    • You will collaborate with the Chief People Officer to understand the product features and benefits, and to align your sales strategy with the company goals.
    • You will create and post appealing recruitment ads on different platforms, and use various sources and methods to reach out to potential candidates.
    • You will screen and assess candidates' needs and interests, and schedule and conduct sales calls with them.
    • You will provide constructive feedback and support to candidates throughout the sales process, and negotiate and close deals with them
    • You will maintain a pipeline of candidates for future sales opportunities, and track and report on your sales performance and results.
    • To be successful in this role, you need to have a bachelor's degree in human resources, business administration, or a related field, and proven experience as a sales recruiter or a similar role.
    • You also need to have knowledge of sales roles, processes, and best practices, and familiarity with various recruitment tools and methods, such as ATS, CRM, etc.
    • You need to have excellent communication and interpersonal skills, and the ability to work independently.
    • You need to have strong organizational and time management skills, attention to detail and quality, and a goal-oriented and results-driven mindset.

    Responsibilities

    • Direct candidates to follow SquareResults on social media platforms such as LinkedIn, Twitter, Instagram, etc.
    • Evaluate our company products and services to create an accurate salesperson profile.
    • Develop and implement a sales strategy in consultation with hiring managers.
    • Determine the audience, method, and sell the product that best meet the candidate need.
    • Design eye-catching recruitment advertisements and post them on various platforms.
    • Source and attract candidates using various channels, such as social media, referrals, job boards, etc.
    • Conduct screening calls and assessments to the candidates' needs.
    • Schedule and coordinate sales call with the candidates.
    • Provide feedback and guidance to candidates throughout the sales process.
    • Negotiate and extend product and services to selected candidates.
    • Maintain a database of potential candidates for future sales opportunities.
    • Track and report on sales metrics and outcomes.

    Requirements

    • Bachelor's degree in human resources, business administration, or related field.
    • Proven experience as a sales recruiter or in a similar role.
    • Knowledge of sales roles, processes, and best practices.
    • Familiarity with various recruitment tools and methods, such as ATS, CRM, etc.
    • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
    • Ability to work independently.
    • Strong organizational and time management skills.
    • Attention to detail and quality.
    • Goal-oriented and results-driven mindset.
